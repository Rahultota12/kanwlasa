About Kanwlasa

According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Kanwlasa village is 474290. Kanwlasa village is located in Khategaon tehsil of Dewas district in Madhya Pradesh, India. It is situated 15km away from sub-district headquarter Khategaon (tehsildar office) and 117km away from district headquarter Dewas. As per 2009 stats, Eklera is the gram panchayat of Kanwlasa village. The total geographical area of village is 1283.62 hectares. Kanwlasa has a total population of 1,978 peoples, out of which male population is 1,016 while female population is 962. Literacy rate of kanwlasa village is 51.47% out of which 60.14% males and 42.31% females are literate. There are about 385 houses in kanwlasa village. Khategaon is nearest town to kanwlasa for all major economic activities, which is approximately 15km away.
